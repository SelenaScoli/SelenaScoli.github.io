About me

Evelyn Casale is an artist who never took her head from the clouds. She was born in 2006, and currently studying at the Escola das Artes e de Imagem (School of Arts and Image). She grew up in a rural small town in the US, going straight from knowing everyone at the local grocery store to moving to Portugal. Ever since she was little she had pen and paper with her wherever she goes.
